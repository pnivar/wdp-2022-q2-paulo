Churaumi Aquarium

On or around December of 2009, I had the opportunity to visit one of the most amazing aquariums in the world. Churaumi Aquarium is located on a remote island south of mainland Japan called Okinawa. by far this has been the best aquarium I've ever visited.

Niagara Falls

On or around February of 2022, I also had the opportunity to visit the Niagara Falls for the first time ever. Located in Upstate, NY right at the US Canadian border the Niagara Falls is among the most visited places by turist around the world.
